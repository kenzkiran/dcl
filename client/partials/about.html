We started as a group of people who share the passion and love for the game of cricket. We take time out of our weekend chores to get together and practice our cricketing skills. While doing so we noticed a lot of other cricket players doing the same and got opportunities to play matches with them.

This lead to the idea of forming a league which would bring all the cricket teams who play taped tennis ball cricket. This would provide an opportunity for all the teams to play more organized cricket and the league to hold regular seasons and tournaments. The teams in the league share the following points:

  • Passion for the game of cricket
  • Playing with taped tennis cricket ball
  • Playing 20/25 limited over matches
  • Playing matches on the weekends

This website is the starting symbol for the implementation of this idea. This website provides all the teams a presence on the internet. They can store their players statistics and match statistics making it accessible to everyone on the internet. This provides the teams with more professional look and feel.
